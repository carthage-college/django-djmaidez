What is this about?

Please take a couple of minutes to let the college know how to get in touch with you in case of an emergency.

Once each semester we will ask everyone to update the information in this form. There are two sections:

  • How should we contact you?
    The college maintains an emergency notification system that allows us to send immediate notification via telephone, text message, and email. These numbers and addresses should be ways to reach you.
  • Whom should we contact about you?
    If we determine that something is wrong, we will try to contact the person or people listed in this section.
